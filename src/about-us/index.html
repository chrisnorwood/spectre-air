About Us

Providing Logistical Solutions For Over 30 Years

Spectre Air & Ground Freight, Inc. was founded in 1985 as a small courier service, primarily serving the greater Bay Area, California. We arranged express frequent deliveries to and from San Diego and Los Angeles (greater southern CA region).

Today, we arrange and manage ground freight for our customers in all 50 US states, Mexico and Canada. If you need to ship by air or ocean, domestically or internationally, we can help arrange solutions for you, as well.

With thousands of loads successfully delivered on the books, on-time and in-shape, you can trust us to take care of your freight.
